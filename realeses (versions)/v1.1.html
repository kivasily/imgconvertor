About IMG Converter

This web application is a highly modern, fully functional client-side photo converter tool. It operates entirely without backend servers, ensuring your uploaded pictures are processed purely within your device memory infrastructure.

Your files are never transmitted across server networks, ensuring total privacy compliance, maximum protection parameters, and extreme speed efficiency natively optimized by browser rendering technologies.
